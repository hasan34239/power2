COMPANY PROFILE :

Rafay Power Solutions. (RPS) is purposely formed to provide the technical Asset Management Services to the financial institutions involved in the Operating business and to institutions / industries interested in outsourcing the Operation and Maintenance of their machinery assets.RPS offers its clients the right blend of Management and Technical expertise in Operation & maintenance of specially but not limited to Power Generation Equipment. Following is the brief profile of our products and services;

.

1. RENTAL DIVISION-POWER GENERATION :

RPS is the asset manager for leading financial institutions and is operating large fleet of generators. Increase in our fleet size day by day shows the satisfaction of our clients and their trust in our products and services. We offer following value added packages to our customers, however we are always open for the customization of our offerings.
o Diesel generators from 40 kVA to 500 kVA on Rental Basis to meet your emergency needs.
o Diesel / Gas Power House from 500 kVA to 3 MW on long term operating lease in collaboration with our Principals. Offer complete turn key solution from supply to installation and from operation to maintenance.

.

RPS deals in sales of brand new generators of Chines, European and Japanese origin. We offer following products ex-stock:
o Exclusive Distributor for Cummins Power Generation, UK Gas business

o 2 kVA to 10 kVA Petrol Generators.
o 12 kVA to 20 kVA Diesel generators from AKSA and ELEMAX, Japan.
